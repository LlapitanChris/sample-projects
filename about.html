"What are we about?"

ICE GAMING is the one-step center for avid video game fans, delivering entertainment to those gamers who are seeking new games and content to play or watch, varying from big triple A games into small indie games that have been developed by individual that has a passion for game development.

The Chronicles of ICE Gaming

Ice Gaming was created on November of 2019 by Ian C. Etruiste. You've probably already guessed where the abbreviation ICE came from, but if you still haven't, no worries the name was based on the creator's initials. The site was intended to be created to test the skills and knowledge that have been acquired on the first 2 weeks of Zuitt's Bootcamp, Little did he know that this project was intended to overall improve the skills and knowledge that was gained but it was also to showcase our passion not just in developing but in our personal interest.
